# INSURANCE RISK ASSESSMENT & CLAIMS ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze insurance policies, claim amounts, customer demographics, vehicle information, and risk patterns. The dashboard enables users to dynamically switch between Total Policies and Total Claim Amount views for comprehensive business analysis.

## Business Problem

Insurance companies need to monitor policy distribution, claim amounts, customer demographics, and risk factors to make informed business decisions. This dashboard provides a centralized view of key insurance metrics and trends.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

## Key KPIs

* Total Policies: 37,542

## Key Insights

- Total Policies analyzed: 37,542
- Total Claim Amount: $187.8M
- Private vehicle usage accounts for the majority of policies and claims.
- Policy distribution is relatively balanced across coverage zones.
- The 36–55 age groups contribute significantly to total policies and claim amounts.
- Claim patterns vary based on education and marital status segments.
